;DAMI3000 — Maze Solver


>>github.com/Maze-Solver-Robot

This Arduino-based project is a comprehensive and robust system featuring over 1300+ lines of code and a wide array of functionalities. It incorporates various modes and automated routines, including a detailed Testing mode, Maze mode, Bluetooth mode, Feedback through leds and buzzers... . This was a PFA(Final Year Project) of the 2nd Preparatory Year, that was graded 17/20 and was rated among the top three projects among all of the classes. More on my LinkedIn/GitHub.

SEBN-MA Sumitomo Electric Bordnetze - Morocco is a Moroccan company that specializes in the manufacturing of automotive wire harnesses and electrical components. These are the complex networks of wires and cables that connect all the electronic parts inside cars.

During my observation internship of 1 month at SEBN-MA, I had the unique opportunity to immerse myself in the world of automotive engineering within the Central Production Engineering 'CPE' department. This experience allowed me to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge gained at EMSI and its practical application in a real-world industrial setting. I gained a deep appreciation for the role of automation and observed SEBNs most high-tech and expensive machinery, understanding the intricate steps involved in manufacturing complex wire harnesses. I also honed essential professional and interpersonal skills by collaborating with a team of engineers , solidifying my career aspirations in the engineering field.
